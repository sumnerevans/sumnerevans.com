LGA-12: Macros
==============

1. Read `Fear of Macros`__ (yes, the *whole thing*). You may also find it
   useful to read `Pattern-Based Macros`__ in the Racket Guide.

2. **After you have read Fear of Macros,** write one interesting macro to share
   with your group.

__ http://www.greghendershott.com/fear-of-macros/all.html
__ http://docs.racket-lang.org/guide/pattern-macros.html
