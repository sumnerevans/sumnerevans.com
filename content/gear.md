## OS and Desktop Environment

I run [NixOS](https://nixos.org/). You can
[view my configs on GitHub](https://github.com/sumnerevans/nixos-configuration/).

I use [Dank Material Shell](https://danklinux.com/) on [Niri](https://niri-wm.github.io/niri/).

I use zsh, neovim, neomutt, and Firefox daily.

## Phone and Watch
